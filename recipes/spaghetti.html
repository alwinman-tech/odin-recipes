Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet. Stir well to combine with the garlic and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss well to ensure that all the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves or grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy your homemade spaghetti!
